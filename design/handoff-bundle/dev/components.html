Buttons

Four roles. Primary (Tussock Gold CTA), accent (Ridge Teal), ghost (outlined), destructive (Peat Ember). All share the same sizing, padding, focus ring, and disabled style.

Focus ring is always 2px solid var(--color-focus-ring) with a 2px offset — never remove it.
